## Part 1: 5-Minute Live Demo Script 🎬

Sabse bada hathiyaar = **chalta hua demo**. 100 baaton se zyada bikta hai. Client dekhta hai → turant samajh jaata hai.

**Setup (meeting se pehle):** Laptop charge, internet strong, demo bot (`/demo`) aur dashboard (`/admin`) dono tab me pehle se khule. Client ki website ka URL haath me.

### Minute 0–1 — Hook (widget dikhao, mat samjhao)

- `/demo` page kholo (salon/dental fake business ka demo bot).
- Widget bubble pe click karo. Chat khul jaata hai.
- **Bolo:** "Ye dekhiye — aapke customer ki tarah main sawaal poochta hoon."
- "Try asking..." me se ek sawaal click karo (jaise *"Kya Sunday ko khula hai?"*).
- Bot turant jawab deta hai. 2 second. **Chup raho, jawab ko bolne do.** 🤫

### Minute 1–2 — Source + Trust (yahan "wow" hota hai)

- Jawab ke neeche **source** dikhao.
- **Bolo:** "Dekho — ye jawab hawa se nahi bana. Ye aapke content se aaya, source ke saath. Isliye ye galat nahi bolta."
- **Samajh lo:** Client ka sabse bada dar = "AI galat bologa." Source dikhate hi ye dar khatam. Ye tumhara trump card hai.

### Minute 2–3 — Lead Capture (paise ka connection)

- Chat me poochho: *"Mujhe appointment chahiye"* / *"Price kya hai?"*
- Bot naam + phone/email maangta hai. Bhar do.
- **Bolo:** "Bas — ye ek lead capture ho gayi. Raat ho ya weekend, ye kabhi so nahi raha."

### Minute 3–4 — Dashboard (client ki value)

- `/admin` tab kholo.
- Leads list dikhao: "Ye rahi wo lead jo abhi aayi."
- Chats, docs upload, embed-copy button jaldi se dikhao.
- **Bolo:** "Har mahine main aapko report bhejunga — is mahine kitni leads aayi. Aapko kuch technical nahi karna."

### Minute 4–5 — THE KILLER MOVE (client ki apni site)

Sabse strong. Isko **hamesha** karo agar time ho.

- Client ki **apni website** ka URL lo. Uske 1-2 public page ka text ingest karo.
- 5-10 min me ek chhota bot ready. (Isliye onboarding flow tez rakha hai.)
- Client se bolo: **"Aap apni site ka koi bhi sawaal poochhiye."**
- Bot uski hi site se jawab deta hai. 👀
- **Samajh lo:** Jab bot uski apni site ka sahi jawab deta hai — uski aankhein chamak jaati hain. Deal yahin band hoti hai. Baaki sab formality hai.

> **Golden rule:** Kam bolo, zyada click karo. Demo bolta hai, tum nahi.

## Part 2: 3 Pain-Point Pitch Lines (verbatim, ready to say) 🎯

Har pitch me ye 3 line bolo. Yaad kar lo — rat lo.

1. **"Aap raat aur weekend me leads miss karte ho — bot 24/7 jawab deta hai, ek bhi customer haath se nahi jaata."**
2. **"Generic bots galat jawab dete hain — hamara sirf aapke content se jawab deta hai, source ke saath. Aapki izzat kharab nahi hoti."**
3. **"Aapke paas tech team nahi hai — hum banate hain, host karte hain, maintain karte hain. Aapko ek button bhi nahi dabana."**

**Samajh lo:** Feature mat ginwao. Pain + result bolo. Client apni problem me apne aap ko dekhta hai — tabhi wallet khulta hai.

## Part 3: Pricing — hours mat becho, result becho 💵

| Kya | Kitna | Isme kya |
|---|---|---|
| **Setup (one-time)** | ₹15,000 – ₹40,000 | Bot banana, branding, docs ingest, site pe lagana, training |
| **Retainer (har mahine)** | ₹8,000 – ₹25,000 | Hosting, API cost, maintenance, chhote tweaks, monthly report |

- Shuru me setup thoda kam rakho (₹15k) taaki pehla review/case-study mile. Review milne ke baad **rate badhao.**
- Videsh client ho to: setup **$200–600**, retainer **$300–1,000/mahina**.
- **Hamesha "build + monthly retainer" offer karo.** Sirf build kabhi mat becho.

**Samajh lo:** Build ek baar paisa deta hai. Retainer **har mahine** deta hai — slow month me bhi. 5 retainer × ₹15k = **₹75,000/mahina, sote hue.** Yehi asli aazadi hai. 💪

**Pricing bolne ka tareeka:** Number confidently bolo, phir **chup raho.** Jo pehle bolega wo haar jaayega. Awkward silence ko sehna seekho.

## Part 4: Objection Handling (client bolega / tum bologe) 🛡️

| Client bolega | Tum bologe |
|---|---|
| "Mahenga hai" | "Ek extra lead per mahina isse zyada kama deta hai. Ye kharcha nahi, kamai hai." |
| "AI galat bologa" | "Isliye hum RAG use karte hain — sirf aapke content se, source ke saath. Abhi demo me dekh liya na?" |
| "Baad me dekhte hain" | "14-din free trial pe laga deta hoon aapki site pe. Leads khud dikhengi, phir faisla karna." |
| "Mera business chhota hai" | "Chhote business ke liye hi best hai — aap har lead pe attend nahi kar sakte, bot kar deta hai." |
| "ChatGPT free hai, wo use kar lunga" | "ChatGPT aapki site nahi jaanta, galat bolta hai, lead capture nahi karta. Ye aapka apna trained bot hai." |
| "Pehle se koi chatbot dekha, kaam nahi kiya" | "Wo generic tha. Ye sirf aapke content se bolta hai — abhi aapki site pe test karke dikhaunga." |
| "Sochkar batata hoon" | "Bilkul. Main 14-din trial live kar deta hoon — sochte-sochte leads aati rahengi. Loss kuch nahi." |

**Samajh lo:** Objection = interest ka signal. Ghabrao mat. Har "na" ke peeche ek chhota dar hai — dar hatao, deal ho jaati hai.

## Part 5: Cold Outreach Templates (pehli meeting ke liye) 📲

Goal sirf ek: **meeting/demo book karna.** Bechna meeting me hoga, message me nahi. Short rakho.

### WhatsApp — Template A (direct + demo offer)

> Namaste [Naam] ji 👋 Main [Tumhara Naam], chhote businesses ke liye AI chat assistant banata hoon jo website pe 24/7 customer sawaalon ka jawab deta hai aur leads capture karta hai.
>
> Aapki [business type] ke liye maine ek chhoti demo bana rakhi hai. 5 min me dikha sakta hoon — aapki apni site se jawab dega. Kal 4 baje ya parso subah, kya theek rahega? 🙂

### WhatsApp — Template B (super short, warm intro)

> Hi [Naam] ji! Aapki website dekhi — customers ke sawaalon ke liye ek 24/7 auto-reply bot laga sakta hoon jo ek bhi lead miss na kare. 2 min ka demo bhej doon? 🙂

### Email — Template C (thoda formal)

> **Subject:** [Business Naam] ke liye 24/7 chat assistant — 5 min demo?
>
> Namaste [Naam] ji,
>
> Main [Tumhara Naam]. Main small businesses ke liye ek AI chat assistant banata hoon jo aapki website pe:
> - Customers ke sawaalon ka **turant jawab** deta hai (raat/weekend bhi)
> - Har visitor ki **lead capture** karta hai (naam + phone)
> - Sirf **aapke content** se bolta hai — galat jawab nahi
>
> Aapki apni website se ek chhoti demo 5 min me dikha sakta hoon. Is hafte 15 min ka time mil sakta hai?
>
> Shukriya,
> [Tumhara Naam] · [Phone] · [Demo link]

**Samajh lo:** Roz 10 message bhejo. 1-2 reply karenge, 1 meeting degi, kuch meetings ke baad 1 client. Numbers ka khel hai — bas rukna mat. Follow-up 2-3 din baad ek baar zaroor karo. 💪

## Part 6: "Client ne YES bola" — Onboarding Checklist ✅

Deal pakki? Ab tez aur professional dikho.

- [ ] Advance lo — **setup ka 50% pehle**, baaki go-live pe. (Full advance best.)
- [ ] Ek chhota written scope bhejo (WhatsApp/email): kya milega, kab, kitne me. Confusion khatam.
- [ ] Client se content lo: website URL, FAQ, service list, pricing, timings.
- [ ] Bot banao — docs ingest, branding (colours/logo), "Try asking..." sawaal set.
- [ ] Test karo: 10 asli sawaal poochho, galat jawab fix karo.
- [ ] Embed code client ki site pe lagao (ya unke developer ko bhejo).
- [ ] Live hone ke baad client ko **5-min walkthrough** do — dashboard, leads kahan aati.
- [ ] Retainer start date + payment reminder set karo (calendar me).

**Samajh lo:** Ek client 2-3 ghante me onboard ho jaata hai. System bana rakha hai — har baar zero se mat socho.

## Part 7: "Delivery ke baad" — Review, Referral, Case-Study 🌟

Yahi se agla client free me milta hai. Delivery ke 1 hafte baad zaroor poochho.

- **Review:** "Kaam pasand aaya? Ek 5-star review Google/Upwork pe de denge? 1 min lagega." ⭐
- **Referral:** "Aapke jaan-pehchaan me koi aur business owner hai jise ye chahiye? Unko bhi laga doon." 🤝
- **Case study:** 1 mahine baad leads ka number lo → *"X salon ne 1 mahine me 30 leads capture kiye."* Ye agli pitch ka proof hai. 📈

**Samajh lo:** Ek khush client = 2-3 naye client. Review + referral maangna bhoolna = paisa table pe chhodna. Har baar poochho, sharmao mat.
