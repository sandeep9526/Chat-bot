# Phase 07 — Client ko Demo + Pitch (Paisa yahan aata hai) 💰

Sab ban gaya — ab **bechna** hai. Ye file technical nahi, business hai. Client ko "AI" nahi, **result** bechna hai. Iski practice karo, ye tumhari sabse important skill hai.

**Time: hamesha — ye roz ka kaam hai**

---

## Task 1: Ek killer live demo taiyaar rakho

Ek public demo bot rakho (jaise `demo.zeva.app`) ek fake business ka (salon/dental). Usme:
- Widget laga ho (asli, ek line se).
- 3-4 "Try asking..." sawaal likhe hon.
- Sources dikhein, lead ticket chale.

**Samajh lo:** Ek chalta hua demo 100 baaton se zyada bikta hai. Client dekhta hai → turant samajh jaata hai. Ye tumhara sabse bada hathiyaar hai.

---

## Task 2: Client ki apni site pe 5-min "wow" demo

Sabse strong move: client ki **apni website** ka URL lo, uski 1-2 public page ki text se ek chhota bot 10 min me bana ke dikhao — "ye aapki hi site se jawab de raha hai."

**Kaise check karein:** Client apni site ka sawaal poochta hai, bot sahi jawab deta hai → uski aankhein chamakti hain. ✅

---

## Task 3: 3 pain points bolo (yehi problem hai)

Har baat me ye 3 line bolo:
1. "Aap raat/weekend me leads miss karte ho — bot 24/7 jawab deta hai."
2. "Generic bots galat jawab dete hain — hamara sirf aapke content se jawab deta hai, source ke saath."
3. "Aapke paas tech team nahi — hum banate, host, aur maintain karte hain. Aapko kuch technical nahi karna."

**Samajh lo:** Client apni problem me apne aap ko dekhega. Feature mat ginwao — pain + result bolo.

---

## Task 4: Pricing — hours mat becho

- **Setup (one-time):** shuru me ₹15,000–40,000 (ya $200–600 pehle review ke liye). Review milne ke baad badhao.
- **Retainer (har mahine):** ₹8,000–25,000 (ya $300–1,000). Isme hosting, API, maintenance, chhote tweaks, monthly report.

Hamesha **"build + monthly retainer"** offer karo. Retainer = har mahine aane wala paisa = tumhari asli aazadi.

**Samajh lo:** Build ek baar paisa deta hai. Retainer har mahine deta hai — slow month me bhi. 5 retainer × ₹15k = ₹75k/mahina, sote hue.

---

## Task 5: Objection ke jawab ready rakho

| Client bolega | Tum bologe |
|---|---|
| "Mahenga hai" | "Ek extra lead/mahina isse zyada kama deta hai. Ye kharcha nahi, kamai hai." |
| "AI galat bologa" | "Isliye hum RAG use karte hain — sirf aapke content se, source ke saath. Demo dekhiye." |
| "Baad me dekhte hain" | "14-din free trial pe laga deta hoon aapki site pe — leads khud dikhengi." |

---

## Task 6: Har client ke baad

- 5-star review maango (Upwork/Google).
- Referral maango: "Aur kisi business owner ko chahiye?"
- Case study banao: "X salon ne 1 mahine me 30 leads capture kiye."
